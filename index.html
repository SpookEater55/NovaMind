My Story

In 2007, at just 13 years old, I fell in love with coding. While most kids were outside playing, I was in front of an old computer teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. That spark never left me.

What started as a hobby quickly became a lifelong passion. Over the years I built dozens of projects, learned countless languages and frameworks, and pushed myself through many late nights of debugging and learning.

One of the highlights of my journey was becoming an early developer on Picoin — a project that gave me invaluable experience in building real systems under pressure during the early days of crypto.

After nearly two decades of continuous learning and building, I created NovaMind. I wanted to combine my love for elegant code with practical, real-world impact — starting with the hospitality industry.

From a curious 13-year-old in 2007 to building AI assistants today — the journey continues.
