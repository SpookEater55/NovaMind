My Journey

In 2007, at just 13 years old, I discovered the magic of coding. While most kids were playing outside, I was teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on an old family computer. That moment changed everything.

What began as curiosity quickly turned into a deep passion. Over the next 18+ years, I immersed myself in technology — building websites, tools, automation systems, and experimental projects across many languages and platforms.

One of the most exciting milestones was becoming an early developer on Picoin. Working on a real crypto project in its early days taught me how to build under pressure, ship quickly, and think about systems that can scale globally.

Those years of relentless learning, countless late nights, and continuous creation led me to found NovaMind. I wanted to create something meaningful — combining elegant code with real-world usefulness, starting with the hospitality industry.

Every feature, every line of code, and every interaction in NovaMind carries the experience of nearly two decades of dedication to building better tools.

From a curious kid in 2007 to creating intelligent AI assistants today — the journey is still going strong.
