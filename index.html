My Journey

In 2007, at just 13 years old, I discovered the magic of coding. While most kids were playing outside, I was teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on an old family computer. That spark never left me.

Over the next 18+ years, I built many projects and became an early developer on Picoin, gaining real-world experience in building scalable systems.

The idea for NovaMind was born from a frustrating personal experience. After a long trip, I arrived at a hotel exhausted, only to wait nearly 45 minutes at the front desk because the staff was overwhelmed. As I stood there, I thought: “There has to be a better way.”

That moment became the foundation. I decided to use my nearly two decades of coding experience to build an intelligent AI assistant that could instantly handle guest communications, reservations, welcome messages, and routine tasks — dramatically reducing wait times and improving service.

NovaMind exists because I experienced the pain of slow service firsthand — and decided to build the solution so no one else has to wait like I did.
